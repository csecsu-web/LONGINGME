What this is

Longingme is a quiet space for people who are struggling but are not ready for therapy, diagnosis, or labels.

It is not here to fix you. It is not here to judge you. It exists for the in-between.

Privacy

We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information.

Private entries are stored only in your browser. Shared entries are anonymous text only — nothing else.

We do not sell data. We do not share data. We do not run ads.

Moderation

Content that instructs or encourages self-harm is filtered automatically. This is protective, not punitive.

You can express pain here. You cannot instruct others to hurt themselves.

What this cannot do

This is not a crisis service. It cannot replace professional support. If you are in danger, please contact someone who can help.
